Who We Are

Centre for Global Health & Bioethics Research (CGHBR) provides independent, high-quality research and consultancy services with a focus on maternal health and ethical practice. We help organisations, researchers and governments make informed, evidence-based decisions that improve health outcomes.

We provide: bespoke research design, policy evaluation, institutional capacity building and hands-on bioethics guidance for research teams.

Our Core Focus Areas

Bioethics Consultancy

Expert ethical guidance for institutions, investigators and organisations working in sensitive health domains — IRB support, ethics frameworks and protocol reviews.

Health Research

Evidence-based research with emphasis on maternal health, public health systems, and interventions that inform policy and practice.

Training & Capacity Building

Workshops, mentorship and structured training for researchers and health professionals on research ethics, protocol development and implementation research.
