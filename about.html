ABOUT CGHBR

Advancing health through ethical research, capacity building and trusted consultancy

Mission & Impact

Our Mission

To strengthen health systems and research conduct through practical, ethics-centered research, training and advisory services.

What we do

  • Design and implement maternal health studies
  • Provide IRB support and ethics reviews
  • Deliver tailored training and toolkits for ethics committees
  • Translate evidence into policy recommendations

Milestones

  1. 2018 — CGHBR established
  2. 2019 — First national maternal health evaluation
  3. 2021 — Multi-region ethics training rollout
  4. 2024 — Published evidence brief adopted by regional health authority
