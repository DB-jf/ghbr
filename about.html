About Us

Advancing ethical health research that changes lives.

The Centre for Global Health & Bioethics Research (GHBR) partners with researchers, policymakers and communities to deliver high quality research and training in bioethics and maternal health. Our work spans applied research, policy guidance and capacity building across Africa and beyond.

Work with us Explore services

Our mission

Our mission is to improve health and advance ethics through rigorous research, pragmatic consultancy and targeted training. We place communities and ethical standards at the center of every project, ensuring that findings translate into policy and practice.

Research Excellence
Methodologically rigorous studies and peer-reviewed outputs.
Bioethics Guidance
Ethical frameworks and policy support for institutions and studies.
Capacity Building
Tailored training courses in maternal health and research ethics.

Services we provide

Applied Research

Design and execution of quantitative and qualitative research projects, including study design, data collection, analysis and publication support.

Bioethics Consultancy

Ethical review support, development of ethics guidelines, and advisory services for institutions and projects with complex ethical considerations.

Training & Workshops

Delivering capacity building in maternal health, research ethics, and responsible conduct of research to professionals and institutions.
