Our Services

High-quality research, ethical guidance and capacity building for institutions, organisations and policymakers.

What We Offer

Bioethics Consultancy

We provide ethical oversight, IRB support, protocol review, research integrity guidance and frameworks that strengthen compliance across institutions.

Health Research

We conduct rigorous research with a focus on maternal health, public health systems, reproductive health and policy-impact evaluation.

Training & Capacity Building

We deliver structured training, workshops and mentorship for ethics committees, researchers and health professionals across Ghana and beyond.

Policy Evaluation

We support public-sector institutions to design, assess and refine policies using evidence-based methodologies grounded in ethical considerations.

Institutional Strengthening

We build capacity through systems audits, ethics governance development and operational strengthening for research-intensive organisations.

Research Advisory & Mentorship

We offer advisory support for investigators, early-career scientists and health policy practitioners working on complex and sensitive research topics.

Work with us for stronger, more ethical research outcomes.

We support both public and private institutions across Africa.
